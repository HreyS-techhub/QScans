Common Questions

This page provides essential information about QScans, your trusted medical scan center including services, location, appointment details, and more

What services does QScans offer?
QScans provides a comprehensive range of medical imaging services, including X-rays, ultrasounds, CT scans, MRI scans, and specialized scans for various medical conditions.
Do I need to schedule an appointment for a scan at QScans?
While we do accept walk-ins, we recommend scheduling an appointment to minimize wait times and ensure prompt service. You can easily book an appointment by calling our center.
Are medical scans at QScans, such as CT or MRI, safe?
Yes, medical scans at QScans are generally safe. Our equipment is meticulously maintained, and our staff is trained to prioritize your safety during the procedure. Please inform our staff of any allergies, medical conditions, or pregnancy before your scan.
How soon can I expect to receive my scan results from QScans?
Your scan results will be interpreted by our experienced radiologists, and the report will be forwarded to your referring physician. The time it takes to receive your results may vary, but we are dedicated to providing timely and accurate reports.
Can I request a copy of my scan images at QScans?
Yes, you can request a copy of your scan images in a format of your choice, such as on a CD or digital media. Please inform our staff during your appointment if you would like a copy.
Where can I direct additional questions or concerns regarding QScans?
If you have any more questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to get in touch with our friendly and knowledgeable staff. You can reach us at 0424-4562222, and we're here to assist you.
